Concept Artist

Immortal Career Concept Artist

Responsibilities

  • Design unit and structure concepts for a AAA Real-Time Strategy game.
  • Sketch, draw, illustrate and paint concepts to clearly and efficiently convey your ideas.
  • Create detailed production art (orthographics, turnarounds, animation sheets) for the rest of the Art Team to turn into game assets
  • Create quick and dirty sketches that successfully communicate a concept’s key features, to efficiently explore different directions
  • Collaborate with different disciplines of the Art, Creative, Gameplay and Tech teams to create concepts that fulfill all required criteria.
  • Create concepts that adapt to specific narrative, tone, style, unit/structure role.
